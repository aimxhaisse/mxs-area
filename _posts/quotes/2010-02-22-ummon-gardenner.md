    Once Ummon asked
    a letter light//
    Are you a gardener?//
    //Yes// it replied\\
    //Why have turnips no roots?\\
    Ummon asked the gardener\
    who could not reply\\
    //Because\\ said Ummon//
    rainwater is plentiful

--- Dan Simmons, The Fall of Hyperion
